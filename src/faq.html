What is the AVS?

The Amateur Valorant Series is a collective of esports enthusiasts who are establishing Valorant’s amateur competitive scene. It looks to establish an arena that displays the talents of all aspiring professional Valorant players.

Are there any rank restrictions?

Whether you’re Iron or Radiant, players of all levels are invited to participate!

Are organizations and professionals allowed to play?

No. We welcome the support of any team or organization, however, any player or team that is salaried or under contract by a professional organization is not allowed to participate unless otherwise stated.

Are there prizes?

At the moment, no. The AVS is strictly volunteer-driven; our staff consists of like-minded individuals who are excited to give the Valorant community a place to hone their skills and become the best on their path to pro. Our primary interest is player driven. In the future there will be prizing and other incentives for players.

Where can I direct questions regarding the tournament?

The best way to contact us is on Discord . Our admins and mods are always available and eager to help!

Where can I receive updates?

Any and all updates will be posted in our Discord as well as our Twitter.
